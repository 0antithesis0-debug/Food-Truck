Our Story

Forged from
Coal Country.

Southeast Kansas didn't just mine coal — it built communities around it. Iron & Embers is our way of carrying that spirit forward: hard-working, fire-forged food rolling through the towns the miners built.

Before the restaurant opens its doors, the truck rolls first. Same flavors, same mining-heritage soul — just smaller, faster, and ready to find you wherever you are in Crawford County and beyond.

Everything is cooked over real hardwood. Nothing is corporate. Every dish name comes from the mine.
